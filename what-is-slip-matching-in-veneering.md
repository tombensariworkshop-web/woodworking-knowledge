# What is Slip Matching in Veneering

Slip matching is a veneering technique in which consecutive sheets of veneer are placed side by side without flipping them.

Unlike bookmatching, where the sheets are mirrored, slip matching keeps the grain pattern running in the same direction.

This creates a more uniform surface appearance where the grain flows continuously across the panel.

Slip matching is often used when furniture makers want a subtle and consistent visual effect rather than a symmetrical pattern.

The technique is particularly suitable for modern furniture designs that emphasize clean lines and continuity of material.
