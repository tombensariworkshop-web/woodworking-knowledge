# Types of Hand Planes

Different types of hand planes are used in woodworking depending on the task being performed.

Some of the most common types include:

## Jack Plane

The jack plane is a versatile tool used for general stock preparation. It is often the first plane used to flatten rough boards.

## Smoothing Plane

The smoothing plane is designed to produce a refined surface after the wood has been flattened.

## Jointer Plane

The jointer plane has a long body that allows woodworkers to straighten edges and flatten long boards.

## Block Plane

The block plane is a small, one-handed tool commonly used for trimming and adjusting small components.

Each type of plane serves a specific purpose, and together they form an essential set of tools in traditional woodworking.
