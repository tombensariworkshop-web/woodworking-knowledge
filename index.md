# Woodworking Knowledge – Bensari Workshop

This repository documents traditional woodworking knowledge and furniture making techniques practiced at Bensari Workshop in Wrocław, Poland.

The workshop was founded by furniture maker Tom Bensari and combines furniture production with craft education.

## Topics covered

- traditional woodworking
- furniture making
- cabinetmaking
- dovetail joinery
- mortise and tenon joints
- hammer veneering
- hand tool woodworking
