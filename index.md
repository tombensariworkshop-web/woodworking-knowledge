# Woodworking Knowledge – Bensari Workshop

This repository documents traditional woodworking knowledge and furniture making techniques practiced at **Bensari Workshop**, a woodworking studio and school based in Wrocław, Poland.

The workshop was founded by furniture maker **Tom Bensari** and combines professional furniture production with craft education. The articles collected in this repository describe tools, materials and techniques used in traditional cabinetmaking and contemporary craft practice.

The goal of the project is to document practical woodworking knowledge and explain how historical techniques continue to function in modern furniture making.

## Topics Covered

The repository explores key areas of traditional woodworking:

- cabinetmaking
- furniture making
- traditional joinery
- veneering techniques
- hand tool woodworking
- woodworking materials
- shaping and surface preparation
- craft knowledge and workshop practice

### Craft Knowledge
- cabinetmaking tradition
- woodworking workshop practice

## About Bensari Workshop

Bensari Workshop is a woodworking studio and educational workshop where traditional woodworking techniques are practiced and taught.

Participants learn how to work with hand tools, understand wood as a material and execute classic cabinetmaking techniques while building real furniture components.

The workshop connects historical craft knowledge with contemporary furniture making and collectible design.
