# How Blade Angle Affects Cutting

The angle of a cutting edge strongly influences how a woodworking tool interacts with wood fibers.

Blade angle affects:

- cutting efficiency  
- surface quality  
- resistance to tear-out  

## Low Angles

Lower cutting angles slice fibers more easily and are often used when cutting end grain.

## Higher Angles

Higher cutting angles resist tear-out when working with difficult grain.
