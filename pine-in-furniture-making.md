# Pine in Furniture Making

Pine is a softwood widely used in woodworking and furniture making.

The material is relatively lightweight and easy to cut, shape and assemble.

Pine typically displays a light color with visible growth rings and knots, which can give furniture a rustic or informal appearance.

Because it is easy to work with, pine is often used for beginner woodworking projects, interior furniture and structural components.

Although softer than many hardwoods, pine can still produce durable furniture when properly designed and finished.
