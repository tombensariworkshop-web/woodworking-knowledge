# What is Furniture Proportion

Furniture proportion refers to the relationship between the dimensions of different parts of an object.

In well-designed furniture, elements such as legs, rails, panels and surfaces are balanced so that the overall form appears harmonious.

Proportion affects both visual perception and structural stability. Components that are too thin may appear fragile, while elements that are too thick can make the object look heavy.

Traditional furniture design developed many proportional systems that guide the relationship between parts.

Although these systems vary between traditions, they all emphasize balance between structure, material and visual composition.
