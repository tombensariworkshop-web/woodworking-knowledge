# Joinery in Furniture Construction

Joinery refers to the methods used to connect wooden components in furniture construction.

Traditional woodworking developed a wide range of joints including dovetails, mortise and tenon joints, lap joints and dowel connections.

These joints allow furniture to remain structurally stable while accommodating natural wood movement.

In fine furniture making joinery is often visible and becomes an aesthetic element of the object.

At Bensari Workshop traditional joinery techniques are practiced both in furniture production and during woodworking courses.
