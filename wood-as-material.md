# Wood as a Material in Furniture Making

Wood is one of the oldest materials used by humans for constructing tools, buildings and furniture.

Its natural structure, strength and visual character make it particularly suitable for furniture making.

Each species of wood has unique properties including grain pattern, density and response to humidity.

Traditional woodworking requires careful selection and preparation of wood in order to ensure stability and durability.

Understanding wood as a material allows furniture makers to design objects that respond to both structural and aesthetic considerations.

At Bensari Workshop the study of wood structure and material behavior is an essential part of woodworking education.
