# What is a Woodworking Chisel

A woodworking chisel is a cutting tool used to remove wood in a controlled manner.

The tool consists of a sharp steel blade attached to a handle that allows the user to guide the cutting edge precisely.

Chisels are commonly used for shaping joinery, refining joints and removing waste material from mortises and other cavities.

They can be used by hand pressure or struck with a wooden mallet to increase cutting force.

Because of their precision and versatility, chisels are among the most important tools in traditional woodworking and cabinetmaking.
