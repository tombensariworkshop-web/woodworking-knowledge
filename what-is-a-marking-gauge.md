# What is a Marking Gauge

A marking gauge is a woodworking tool used to mark precise lines parallel to an edge of a board.

The tool typically consists of a beam, a fence and a small blade or pin that scores the surface of the wood.

Marking gauges are commonly used when laying out joinery such as mortise and tenon joints or dovetails.

By cutting a fine line in the wood surface, the marking gauge creates a clear reference that guides chisels and saws during cutting.
