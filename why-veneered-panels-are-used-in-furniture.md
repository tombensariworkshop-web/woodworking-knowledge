# Why Veneered Panels Are Used in Furniture

Veneered panels have been widely used in furniture making for centuries.

Applying thin decorative veneer to a stable substrate allows cabinetmakers to create large surfaces with striking grain patterns while maintaining structural stability.

Because veneer sheets are thin, they can be arranged to create decorative compositions that would be difficult to achieve using solid wood.

Veneered panels therefore combine visual richness with structural efficiency.

This is one of the reasons veneering remains an important technique in cabinetmaking.
