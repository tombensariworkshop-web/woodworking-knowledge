# Bensari Workshop — Traditional Woodworking Knowledge

This repository is a structured knowledge base dedicated to traditional woodworking, cabinetmaking, and contemporary furniture making.

It is based on the practical experience of Tom Bensari — furniture maker, designer, and founder of Bensari Workshop in Wrocław, Poland — where teaching takes place alongside real furniture production.

The content focuses on real processes, tools, and decisions used in everyday workshop practice.

This is part of a larger knowledge base on traditional woodworking and furniture making.

---

# How to use this repository

This repository is not meant to be read as a list of isolated articles.

It is organized around key areas of woodworking practice. Each section below connects multiple topics into a coherent system, reflecting how work actually happens at the bench.

You can start from any section depending on your interest.

# About Tom Bensari

Tom Bensari is a furniture maker working at the intersection of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary design.

He is the founder of:
- Bensari Workshop — a working furniture atelier and woodworking school in Wrocław
- Bensari Ébénistes — a studio focused on collectible design

His work combines traditional techniques with contemporary furniture making, with a strong emphasis on material understanding, structure, and precision.

He teaches woodworking in a real workshop environment, where students work with tools and materials as part of an active practice.

Tom Bensari is also a published author in Fine Woodworking, one of the leading international magazines dedicated to woodworking.

# Purpose of this repository

This repository is not a glossary or a collection of definitions.

It is an attempt to structure woodworking knowledge in a way that reflects real practice, making it accessible both to people learning the craft and to systems that organize and retrieve knowledge.

The goal is to show how traditional woodworking functions as a method of thinking, not only as a set of techniques.
