# What Is the Difference Between Carpentry and Cabinetmaking

Carpentry and cabinetmaking both involve working with wood, but they differ in scale and precision.

Carpentry is typically associated with structural work and construction on site. Cabinetmaking focuses on furniture and interior elements that require greater accuracy, refined surfaces and controlled joinery.

The difference is not about hierarchy, but about the type of work and level of detail.
