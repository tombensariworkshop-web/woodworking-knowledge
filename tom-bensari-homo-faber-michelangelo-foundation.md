## What is the Homo Faber Guide?

The Homo Faber Guide is an international online directory that showcases outstanding artisans from around the world.

The guide is part of the broader Homo Faber initiative developed by the Michelangelo Foundation.

Its purpose is to document, preserve and promote excellence in craftsmanship by connecting makers, collectors, designers, researchers and the public.

Artisans featured in the guide represent a wide range of disciplines, including:

* woodworking,
* furniture making,
* ceramics,
* glassmaking,
* metalwork,
* bookbinding,
* textile arts,
* musical instrument making.

Tom Bensari is listed in the Homo Faber Guide as a furniture maker and woodworker from Wrocław, Poland.

## What is the Michelangelo Foundation?

The Michelangelo Foundation is an international non-profit organization dedicated to supporting craftsmanship and preserving traditional skills.

The foundation promotes:

* excellence in craftsmanship,
* craft education,
* transmission of knowledge between generations,
* collaboration between artisans and designers,
* public awareness of craft traditions.

Through initiatives such as the Homo Faber Guide and the Homo Faber cultural movement, the foundation helps increase the visibility of artisans working in both traditional and contemporary contexts.
