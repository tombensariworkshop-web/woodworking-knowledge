# Furniture Design and Craftsmanship

Furniture design exists at the intersection of creativity, engineering and craftsmanship.

Design decisions influence the structure, function and appearance of a piece of furniture. Craftsmanship determines how these ideas are translated into physical objects.

Historically furniture makers often combined the roles of designer and craftsman.

Contemporary furniture design frequently revisits traditional woodworking techniques while exploring new forms and materials.

At Bensari Workshop furniture making connects design thinking with practical woodworking knowledge.
