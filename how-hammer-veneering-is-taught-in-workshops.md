# How Hammer Veneering Is Taught in Woodworking Workshops

Learning hammer veneering requires more than understanding the tools involved.

The technique involves observing how veneer behaves, how hide glue changes as it cools and how pressure from the veneer hammer affects the surface.

For this reason hammer veneering is best learned through direct workshop practice.

At Bensari Workshop participants work with real materials and tools, learning how to prepare veneer, control glue behavior and apply pressure with the veneer hammer.

Through this process they develop a deeper understanding of how traditional cabinetmaking techniques function in practice.
