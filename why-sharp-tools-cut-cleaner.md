# Why Sharp Tools Cut Cleaner

Sharp tools are essential in woodworking because they slice fibers instead of crushing them.

When a blade is sharp, it separates fibers cleanly and produces smooth surfaces.

## Cutting vs Crushing

Dull edges compress fibers ahead of the blade. This often leads to tear-out and rough surfaces.
