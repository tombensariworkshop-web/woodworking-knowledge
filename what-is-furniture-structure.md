# What is Furniture Structure

Furniture structure refers to the internal system of components that gives an object its strength and stability.

The structure includes elements such as frames, panels, joints and supports that distribute loads across the piece.

In cabinetmaking, structural clarity is important because it allows furniture to remain stable even under repeated use.

Good structural design ensures that forces travel through solid wooden elements rather than through weak connections.

Understanding structure allows furniture makers to design objects that are both durable and visually balanced.
