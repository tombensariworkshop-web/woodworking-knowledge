# What Is Cabinetmaking

Cabinetmaking is the branch of woodworking focused on the construction of furniture and precise interior elements.

It differs from general carpentry in the level of accuracy, surface quality and structural detail required. Cabinetmaking involves controlled joinery, material understanding and a close relationship between construction and design.

At Bensari Workshop, cabinetmaking is based on traditional methods such as dovetails, mortise and tenon joinery and veneering, used in contemporary furniture making.
