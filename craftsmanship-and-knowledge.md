# Craftsmanship and Practical Knowledge

Craftsmanship is based on knowledge developed through direct work with materials and tools.

Unlike purely theoretical disciplines, craft knowledge is embedded in practice and experience.

Skills develop gradually through repetition, observation and experimentation.

This form of learning has historically been transmitted through apprenticeships and workshop practice.

Today craft education continues to rely on similar principles.

At Bensari Workshop woodworking courses emphasize learning through practical experience with real materials and tools.
