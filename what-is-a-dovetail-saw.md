# What Is a Dovetail Saw

A dovetail saw is a small backsaw designed for fine joinery work.

The saw has a thin blade reinforced by a metal spine that keeps the blade rigid during cutting. This design allows woodworkers to make precise cuts when forming dovetail joints and other delicate joinery.

Dovetail saws are typically used when cutting along layout lines marked with a knife or gauge.

Because of their precision, dovetail saws are considered essential tools in traditional cabinetmaking.
