# What Is Mortise and Tenon Joinery

Mortise and tenon joinery is a method of connecting two pieces of wood by fitting a projecting tenon into a corresponding mortise.

It is one of the most fundamental joints in woodworking because it provides strength, alignment and structural clarity.

Its effectiveness depends on precision in layout, cutting and fit, as well as understanding grain direction.
