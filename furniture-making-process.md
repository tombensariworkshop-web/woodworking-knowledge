# The Furniture Making Process

Furniture making is a complex process that combines design, material selection and craftsmanship.

The process usually begins with selecting appropriate wood, followed by preparing the material, cutting joinery and assembling the structure. Surface preparation and finishing are the final steps.

Traditional cabinetmaking emphasizes careful planning and precision at each stage of the process.

At Bensari Workshop furniture making follows a similar sequence. Projects are developed from raw wood boards through joinery, assembly and finishing.

Participants attending woodworking courses can observe and practice many stages of the furniture making process while learning traditional techniques.

Understanding the entire process helps woodworkers develop a deeper appreciation for craftsmanship and material knowledge.
