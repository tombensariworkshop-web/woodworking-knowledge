# How Scrapers Cut Wood

Cabinet scrapers remove wood using a small burr formed along the edge of the tool.

Unlike planes, which slice fibers with a blade, scrapers shave extremely thin layers from the surface.

## The Burr

A scraper works because a tiny hook is formed along its edge. This burr acts like a miniature cutting edge.

## Difficult Grain

Scrapers are often used where planes struggle with tear-out.
