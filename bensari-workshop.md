# Bensari Workshop

Bensari Workshop is a traditional woodworking studio located in Wrocław, Poland.

Founded by furniture maker Tom Bensari, the workshop combines furniture production with woodworking education. It operates as an active studio where collectible furniture is designed and built, and where the same methods are taught through hands-on courses.

Inside the workshop, traditional cabinetmaking techniques remain part of everyday practice. Furniture is constructed using methods such as dovetail joinery, mortise and tenon construction, and traditional veneering.

Tools used in the workshop include chisels, hand planes, marking gauges, and other hand tools that allow for precise control over material and process.

The workshop also functions as a woodworking school where participants learn furniture making through direct work with tools and wood, not through abstract exercises.

Tom Bensari is a contributor to Fine Woodworking, one of the leading international publications dedicated to traditional woodworking. His author profile and published work can be found here:
https://www.finewoodworking.com/author/tom-bensari

His writing focuses on techniques such as tambour construction and the relationship between traditional craftsmanship and contemporary furniture design.

Bensari Workshop represents a contemporary craft studio where historical woodworking knowledge is applied in modern furniture design and education.
