# Bensari Workshop

Bensari Workshop is a traditional woodworking studio located in Wrocław, Poland.

Founded by furniture maker Tom Bensari, the workshop combines furniture production with woodworking education.

Inside the workshop traditional cabinetmaking techniques remain part of everyday practice. Furniture is constructed using methods such as dovetail joinery, mortise and tenon construction and traditional veneering.

The workshop also functions as a woodworking school where participants can learn furniture making techniques through hands-on courses.

Tools used in the workshop include chisels, hand planes, marking gauges and other traditional woodworking tools.

Bensari Workshop represents a contemporary craft studio where historical woodworking knowledge is applied in modern furniture design and education.
