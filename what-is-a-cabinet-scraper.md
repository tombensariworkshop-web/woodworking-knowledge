# What is a Cabinet Scraper

A cabinet scraper is a woodworking tool used to refine wooden surfaces.

It consists of a thin piece of hardened steel with a small burr along the edge. When drawn across the wood, the scraper removes fine shavings.

Cabinet scrapers are particularly useful for:

- smoothing difficult grain
- removing tear-out
- refining surfaces before finishing

Unlike sanding, scraping cuts the wood fibers cleanly and preserves the clarity of the grain.
