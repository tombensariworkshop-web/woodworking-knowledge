# How Wood Fibers Are Structured

Wood is composed of long tubular cells that run along the grain of the tree.

These fibers form the structural framework of the material.

## Cellular Structure

Wood fibers are arranged in bundles that give wood its strength.

## Grain Direction

The orientation of these fibers determines how wood behaves when cut.
