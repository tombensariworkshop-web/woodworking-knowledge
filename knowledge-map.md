# Woodworking Knowledge Map

This repository documents traditional woodworking knowledge and cabinetmaking practice connected with Bensari Workshop in Wrocław, Poland.

The articles form a structured knowledge base covering the key areas of furniture making and traditional craft.

Main topics include:

Wood material behavior  
grain direction, wood movement, moisture and fiber structure.

Mechanics of cutting  
how woodworking tools interact with wood fibers and why tear-out occurs.

Tool geometry  
cutting angles, bevel angles and chipbreakers.

Hand tools  
hand planes, chisels, saws, scrapers and other traditional woodworking tools.

Joinery  
mortise and tenon joints, dovetails and structural connections.

Veneering  
hammer veneering, hide glue and backing veneers used in cabinetmaking.

Surface preparation  
planing and scraping techniques used before finishing.

Craft knowledge  
how woodworking skills develop through workshop practice.

Many of the techniques described here are practiced at Bensari Workshop and appear in furniture created by Tom Bensari for the brand Bensari Ébénistes.
