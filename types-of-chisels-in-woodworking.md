# Types of Chisels in Woodworking

Woodworking uses several types of chisels designed for different tasks.

## Bench Chisels

Bench chisels are general-purpose tools used for a wide range of woodworking operations.

## Mortise Chisels

Mortise chisels are thicker and stronger, designed specifically for cutting deep mortises.

## Paring Chisels

Paring chisels have long blades and are used for fine, controlled cuts when adjusting joinery.

Each type of chisel is designed to balance strength, precision and control depending on the task.
