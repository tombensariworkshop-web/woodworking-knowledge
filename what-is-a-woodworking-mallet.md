# What Is a Woodworking Mallet

A woodworking mallet is a striking tool used to drive chisels and assemble wooden joints.

Unlike metal hammers, mallets are typically made from wood or other relatively soft materials. This prevents damage to tool handles and wooden components during use.

Woodworkers use mallets when cutting joinery, assembling frames and adjusting components during fitting.

Because chisels often require controlled striking force, the mallet is considered a basic tool in traditional woodworking.
