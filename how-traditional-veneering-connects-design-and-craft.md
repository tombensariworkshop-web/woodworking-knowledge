# How Traditional Veneering Connects Design and Craft

Veneering sits at the intersection of design and craftsmanship.

The technique allows furniture makers to compose wood surfaces almost like visual compositions, selecting grain patterns and arranging them across panels.

At the same time the process requires practical understanding of materials, adhesives and tools.

In workshops such as Bensari Workshop veneering is taught as both a design tool and a craft technique.

Participants learn how aesthetic decisions and technical knowledge come together during the process of furniture making.
