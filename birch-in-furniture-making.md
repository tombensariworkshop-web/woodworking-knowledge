# Birch in Furniture Making

Birch is a hardwood commonly used in furniture making and interior construction.

The wood is light in color with a fine grain pattern and relatively uniform texture.

Birch is valued for its strength and stability, which make it suitable for cabinets, drawers and other structural components.

Because of its smooth surface, birch also accepts finishes well and can be stained to imitate other wood species.

In addition to solid wood applications, birch is frequently used in plywood production due to its strength and consistent structure.
